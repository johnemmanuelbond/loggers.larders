The [[Harport]] Cartographer's guild is a relatively small organization of wayfinders and seafarers who aim to map out the newly discovered lands of [[Nordwythe]]. While their clientele mostly consists of the city's government and guilds (who sometimes have less-than-noble purposes), their explicit goals are to document Nordwythe in it's unmarred state, inlcuding it's breadth of resources, the sovereignty of its locals, and the beauty of its natural features.
