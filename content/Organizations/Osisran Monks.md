Based on the duality between Melian and Morian, this faith admonishes extremes and preaches a path of moderation and justice. Just as civilization must calm the chaos of the natural world, the natural world is owed reverance by civilization.

### The Faith of Melian and Morian
The central myth of the Osisrans is that the god morian sits on his moon and is responsible for quelling the violent throws of Melian after the arrival of Pan.

As such Morian is seen as the protector of mortals, and their shepherd once they've left Melian's "womb"
