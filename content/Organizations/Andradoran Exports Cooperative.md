The AEC (Andradoran Exports Cooperative) is the city's largest organization and manages every voyage to and from [[Andradora]]. They focus on shipping rare [[Nordwythe|northern]] goods like crafts by [[Northern Dwarves]] and pelts from [[Aire Nomads]] to the ever-hungry markets on [[Mardwythe]].

The AEC keeps a tight grip on [[Harport]] politics, a common saying is that "they own half of the New Haven and the Diet too!"

### Main Offices and Dockyards
 They maintain a campus on the northern top of Harport proper with various administrative buildings and staff apartments. They also maintain a ferry to and from the [[Guild Quarter|New Haven]], which they effectively control half of. The city watch is present here, but outnumbered by outsourced AEC security.
