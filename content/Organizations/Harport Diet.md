A 201-member council, this body is the governing force in [[Harport]]. Their responsibility is to collect tariffs, maintain order and safety, conduct foreign policy, and generally advance the city's standing globally.

The diet is split mostly between two factions, the Porters and the Footers. The Porters mostly represent the interests of commerce and the [[Andradoran Exports Cooperative|AEC]] and oppose overregulation. The Footers represent the interests of local residents and swing with whatever populist moment hits the city.
