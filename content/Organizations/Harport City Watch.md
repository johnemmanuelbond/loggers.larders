###### Parents: [[Harport Diet]]
Denoted by green tunics and a leather armor emblazoned with the famous bow and keel, the [[Harport]] City Watch is empowered by the [[Harport Diet|Diet]] to keep order. Their main offices and barracks are located on the Citadel and contain enough housing to accommodate all 800 members (though about half hold a private residence throughout the city).

### Ranks and Offices
- **Chief**: Directly elected by the people, this office is currently held by [[Jesper Forthwright]], and is, more often then not, held by one of the Diet representatives.
- **Inspector**: Two inspectors, (one for birdfoot and one for Harport Proper) are promoted from the pool of current/former Sargents and Detectives by the Chief. The inspectors are responsible for overseeing Sargents and Detectives, interfacing with the Chief and Diet, and handling special requests from organizations. Each inspector gets 3 clerks to help with adminstration.
- **Sargent**: Appointed by internal electrions, the Sargents report directly to the inspectors, organize watch patrols, and handle special requests from citizens. Each sargent is appointed a clerk to help with paperwork. 
- **Detective**: Promoted due to exceptional work on patrol, detectives are placed on specific tasks by either Sargents or Inspectors.
- **Private**: Hired on a need basis, privates undergo 1 month training at the barracks before placed on patrol.
