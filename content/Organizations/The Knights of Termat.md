###### Parents: [[The Solar Church]]
Dedicated to [[Pan|Termat]] (a face of [[Pan|Amat]]), the goddess who guides blazing and righteous moon of Panain, these knights are dedicated to protecting the settlers of [[Harport]] in the name of their sacred [[Andradora|Andradoran]] heritage.

These knights are only respectful of other worshippers of T[[he Solar Church]], acolytes of the [[Osisran Monks]], or those who bear the dignitas of the Andradoran government.
