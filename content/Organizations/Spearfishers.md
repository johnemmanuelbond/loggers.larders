The premier smuggling guild out of [[Harport]], who specialize in unique items controlled tightly by the AEC (egarthopian seeds, dwarven craft, Aire alcohol, etc).

They recognize each other by a pin of a pierced trout pinned somewhere on their clothing. The pin is actually enchanted so as not to be forgeable, though this is only known by the mysterious leader.
