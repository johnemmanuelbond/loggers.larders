###### Parents: [[Osisran Monks]]
The devotees of [[Sashelas]] are worshippers of the freedom of the open sea. Orginally founded in [[Andradora]], the central tenet of the faith is to live as an exemplar of Sashelas, an ancient hero from the pre-Andradoran cultures on [[Mardwythe]].

While the veneration of Sashelas is mostly practiced in port cities either side of [[The Glinting Straits]], sailors are a wide-ranging people and shrines have even made it as far north as [[Harport]] and [[Weir's Point]].
