*This set of calipers is made of shining rune-encrusted golden metal and gives off a conjuration aura.*

The calipers are immaculately tuned and give perfect measurements of distances less than 8 inches. A third point extends a little more than a third of the way between the main two.

*The calipers themselves have no magical ability, but appear to be a component of a larger magical apparatus/ritual.*
