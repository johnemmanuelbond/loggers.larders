###### Parent: [[Harken's Sound]]
Founded over 200 years ago with the coming of [[Harken Keelmender]], the city of Harport sprawls out in front of you. From your vantage point on the bridge you see rolling farmland and pasture situated between two rivers which conjoin at a large bay. The first building you see is a long oval building with towers and bandstands at the front and back. This building, familiar to [[Rivka]], is the famous [[Harport Racetrack]]. Behind the track and to the right you see Shae's stream split into two outlets which surround an island jutting out into the glistening bay. The island itself is built up with a beautiful patchwork of stone and wooden buildings, surrounded by several large dockyards with fishing and trading ships moored within. Behind and to the left of the racetrack you see a peninsula reach out into the bay where three short fingers house even larger docks. A meandering and confusing layout of wooden houses cover this peninsula before a few notably taller stone buildings peek out of the sprawl near those docks. Looking across the bay you can see The whole bay itself is periodically dotted with little settlements and smaller dockyards as it seems this whole body of water is open for trade. Indeed, Harport is a trader's town. The denizens here make their living off of exporting valuable northern goods, lumber, pelts, whale oil, and [[Northern Dwarves|Dwarven]] craft back to [[Andradora]] on one of the periodic vessels which sail Harken's route back to the mainland.

## Demographics
Harport is about 75% [[Andradorans]] emigre, 20% [[Aire Nomads]] and 5% [[Northern Dwarves|Dwarven]]. The Aire are mostly concentrated in the [[Fish Market]] and the Ag Sector. Andradorans dominate the [[Birdfoot]], [[Town Center]], and the [[Old City]]. Dwarves are mostly centralized around the [[Guild Quarter]], where they make up a strong percentge of the [[Harport Mason's Guild|mason's]] and smith's guilds.

## Government
Harport runs on an Andradoran province model: the representative members are randomly selected for a term of three years (no term limit). Every year, a third (67) of these representatives are reselected from the pool of citizens who submit their name at the [[The Citadel|Counsel's Campus]]. These selections are overseen by a committee of 11 members appointed by the diet from their own ranks.
