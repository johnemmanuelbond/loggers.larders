###### *Female, [[Harport Colonials]]/[[Humans]], 56*
###### Location(s): [[Milton]]
The austere judge of [[Milton]].

June is a tall woman with a dark complexion and dressed in a black robe with strong shoulderpads. Her greying black hair curls out of a small black venetian-style hat which sits atop her sharp and angular face.

June is responsible for overseeing relations between the locals and the [[Andradoran Exports Cooperative]] office, ensuring the AEC get's the lumber it needs and no undue pressure comes to Milton.

June is also responsible for ruling on criminal cases, and allocating resources to the local citizens, a task she often delegates to [[Moser Wilderly]].
