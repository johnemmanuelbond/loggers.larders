###### *Female, [[Aire Nomads]], 68*
###### Location(s): [[Milton]]
An elderly Aire woman who most commonly wears a simple brown dress and a smock, Old Gran runs the apothecary in Milton. She mostly stays secluded but is known to host [[Esmellia Leek]] and [[Prim Vosdottir]] for tea on occasion, as well as [[June Sturgil]] and [[Wilson Fit]]. She also provides horsekeeping advice and guidance to [[Silvoy Soot]].

She is known to dress up in traditionally southern Aire festival garb--a red sash and dress, embroidered with blue waves, and a long necklace strung with yellow canary feathers--on festival days, and plays the part of the [[Old Hag]] in the yearly play.

#### Upbringing
Gran is a born-and-bred Southern Aire from [[Milton]]. Her family had always had contact with the other southern Aire out in the forest and had used those contacts to sell pelts to the [[Harport Colonials]] of Milton. However, once the [[Andradoran Exports Cooperative|AEC]] started expanding their influence in Milton, they initiated their own ties with the sourthern Aire, and Gran's family lost their prominence. Taking this as a queue, Gran set out into the world.

#### Adult Life
Gran has actually travelled much of the continent. With a few Aire friends, she explored the decrepit temples at [[Weir's Point]] , canoed down the river [[Wyfloe Delta|Wyfloe]], viewed [[Shimmer Grove]], and even made pilgrimage to the salt caves of her kin up north. All the while she was taking copious notes on the flora and fauna of eastern [[Nordwythe]] so that there would be some kind of catalogue of the indigenous species before the land starts to change under AEC pressure. This knowledge allowed her to set up an apothecary's shop in [[Harport]], but after several years she realized that Milton  was a stabler location for such a purpose, no [[Andradora|Andradoran]] mages to compete with.

#### History with Rivka
Every few years Old Gran visits the chariot tracks in Harport to check in on an old acquaintance.. but really she went just to double check they're treating their horses right. After all, Gran is a part of why this fellow had horses to race in the first place, but that's a story for a different time. Anyway, this contact had recently passed and his son was not as afraid of Gran as his father. So the son started adopting his competitors' breeding and training methods on his horses, and this would not do. So gran decides to scare some sense into the boy and pretend to be the Old Hag of the woods, back from the grave to wreak terrible vengeance. However, before she could spring her trap, [[Rivka]] enacted her own plan. Rivka had grown up with the boy in the villages encircling central Harport. She knew the boy was a bad apple from the neighborhood daycare, torturing bees, stealing his sister's dolls, etc. Rivka engineered a daring escape for these horses, and was so was just as surprised to see Gran transform back into an Aire as the boy would have been. The meeting proved serendipitous as gran noted her natural magical gifts and love for the wilds and recommended she study the ways of nature out in Milton, away from the bustle of Harport.
