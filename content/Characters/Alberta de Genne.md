###### *Female, [[Andradorans]], 102* 
###### Location(s): [[Wyfloe Delta]], [[Harport]], [[Harken's Sound]]
A scholar from [[Andradora]], and prominent member of the [[Knowing Shepherds]]. Alberta is a resourceful and surprisingly durable field agent. Hearing tales of ancient ruins on [[Nordwythe]] (before [[Fort Solton]] bought off the journalists), she packed up and moved to investigate.
