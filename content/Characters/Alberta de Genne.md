###### *Female, [[Andradorans]], 38*
###### Location(s): [[Wyfloe Delta]], [[Harport]], [[Harken's Sound]]
A scholar from [[Andradora]], and prominent member of the [[Knowing Shepherds]]. Alberta is a resourceful and surprisingly durable field agent. Hearing tales of ancient ruins on [[Nordwythe]] (before [[Fort Solton]] bought off the journalists), she packed up and moved to investigate.

Alberta is halfling in the prime of her life! She has a boisterous and bubbly personality and an intense zeal for adventure coupled with a snarky sense of humor. She has round features, fair skin, and light blonde hair. She's wearing khaki pants and a shirt with a chunky black belt and a cute little green canvas hat.
