### Plot Hook

The other players are going to find you imprisoned/unconscious in a previously unexplored ruin. We need to brainstorm:

1) What are you doing there? - probably explored somewhere you shouldn't
2) How long have you been there?
3) What's been keeping you from leaving?


### Possible 'hometowns'

	1. *features*: Play more into the sociopolitical wing of the campaign and align with the whole party's immediate goals.
	2. *constraints*: not much high-tech, if any. Can't really collaborate w/ Drew at all.
2. [[Eitheloth]]: A fading power holds onto the last vestiges of its glory.
	1. features: technologically advanced so plenty of room for artificer tech, options to RP with Sarah's plot.

3. [[Karrid]]: A squalid city of thieves, beggars, and political exiles on the fringes of an otherwise mighty theocracy.
	1. *features*: definitely plenty of bootleg artificer tech, and the location is pretty funny if we go there. Options to RP w/ Isaac's plot.
	2. *constraints*: I'll have to build out more of this side of the continent, and there's no high magic. Think home-alone style booby traps and such.
4. [[Elga Elluin]]: a mist-covered ruin where hermits research esoteric rites for as long as their sanity lasts.
	1. features: attracts intellectuals, especially reckless ones. Options for interlacing story w/ Drew. Relaxed ancestry-requirements.
	2. constraints: either your character is actively losing their mind, or originates in the distant past. Both of which are a challenge RP-wise.
