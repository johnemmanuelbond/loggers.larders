###### Female, [[Andradorans]], 124
###### Location(s): [[Harport]]
An old and senile Andradoran Magus, Andreth has long abandoned the pursuit of power that defined her youth. These days she lives in a quaint apartment in the Market Square with her [[Hagop Koshgarian|protege]]. Andreth is an ancient-looking woman with shock-white hair and a maroon and gold shawl. Her green eyes appear clouded on most days, though she occasionally manifests her formerly piercing gaze.

During her youth, Andreth was a transmutation wizard of medium-high rank in the court of the mage-king, only an attache to her own [[TEMP Andreth's Master|master]] on his council. Never quite as powerful as her master wanted, Andreth was eventually overshadowed by a younger student and relegated to magical item crafting. Shortly thereafter, Andreth's old master was... pruned... by the jealous mage-king and Andreth was once again thrown into the political ring, barely surviving this renewed purge, a now-elderly and mostly-forgotten Andreth was able to quietly slip away on a ship to Harport to live out the rest of her days in peace.

Here in [[Harport]], as her mind slips in and out of lucidity she passes time by experimenting with local cuisine, creating and selling pottery, and occasionally taking a student in secret.
