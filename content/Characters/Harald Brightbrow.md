###### *Male, [[Andradorans]], 63*
###### Location(s): [[Harken's Sound]], [[Fort Solton]]
Dispatched from the main temple to [[Pan|Amat]] in [[Andradora]], Harald commands the [[Knights of Termat]] at [[Fort Solton]]. A tall, silver-haired, cream-skinned man with dark eyes and round features, it's impossible to ignore his air of command given his golden-filigried steel plate armor. Harald distrusts the religious freedom in [[Harken's Sound]] and only trusts the influence of the [[Osisran Monks]] and the [[Solar Church]]. This distrust turned into diabolical scheming when he entered the [[Ancient Merlings|Merling]] ruin underneath Solton that the previous grandmasters had kept secret and sealed off. In this ruin he was posessed by a malevolent spirit who amplified his paranoia and ambition to control Harken's Sound. Now he is in league with [[Grendelle the Grotesque]] to vilify the [[Aire Nomads|Aire]] and seize power in [[Harport]].
