###### *Male, [[Harport Colonials]], 41*
###### Location(s): [[Harport]],[[Birdfoot]],[[Harport Racetrack]]
Swarmy owner of the Harport racetrack, Frank is nonetheless well-kept and fit. He wears a black suit with pinstripes and a tasteful brimmed hat. His face is pleasant and plump, betraying a generally scummy and self-possessed attitude.

While he's soon to be married to [[Ophelia Berendsen]], he's only tangentially connected to her organization. His racetrack is inherited and the day to day is mostly managed by [[Simon Redmont]] while Frank himself covorts with the city's wealthy class. Frank used to be fairly upstanding, but has been thoroughly corrupted by money.
