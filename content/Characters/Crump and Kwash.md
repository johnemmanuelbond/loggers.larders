###### Male(s),[[Aire Nomads]] & [[Harport Colonials]], 57 & 49
###### Location(s): [[Harport]]
These two druids run the day-to-day associated with the ravenry in [[Harport]]'s Market Square.

Felix Crump: an elder Aire druid, Felix's hunched form betrays his age. His smile lines run deep and do nothing to distract from his mischievous and witty personality. Felix wears a simple hide tunic and trousers typical of a southern Aire.

Oscar Kwash: is a straight-shooter. A similarly old halfling in a cute green vest and fashionable pantaloons, Oscar takes this ravenry very seriously and seems himself, and Felix, as performing a duty crucial to the city's function.
