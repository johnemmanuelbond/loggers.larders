###### *Female, [[Andradorans]], 36*
###### Location(s): [[Milton]], [[Silverspire]]
A strikingly beautiful golden-skinned tiefling with black horns and pointed teeth, but no tail. Esmellia runs the [[Startled Starling Inn]]--which mostly caters to [[Harport]] officials and [[Fort Solton]] soldiers travelling to and from Milton, as well as other travellers. Esmellia is a bit of a mystery around town, as she only moved in about 15 years ago, but her kindly nature and soft-spoken charm have earned her many admirers. While working, she wears a black pantsuit and white undershirt to compliment her features, and she seems to have endless energy as she bounces around between the bar, the concierge, and the kitchen.

#### Background
Esmellia Leek (Esme Landros) is the daughter and scion of a prestigious magical bloodline in [[Andradora]] and a direct descendant of a famous _matriarchal elder_ from a few centuries back. Her early years consisted of private tutors, banquets, and dances before she attended mage's college in [[Silverspire]]. There, she continued to hone her skills and power while learning about the Andradoran political system. Esmellia graduated *mage's college* with a perfect pedigree and was primed to enter a bright political career serving the council of elders as a political aide. However this all changed when Esmellia was engaged, against her will, to marry [[TEMP Esmellia's Fiance]] as part of a political alliance with a rival house. Despite pleas from her mother and promises of the power this alliance would bring, Esmellia couldn't bring herself to do it and ran off, eventually meeting [[Fleance Flycatcher]] on a boat to [[Nordwythe]].<br>*(Note: In andradora, magically-induced fertility is possible so there's no ageism when it comes to wifing up.)*

#### Founding the Startled Starling
Meeting Fleance on the ship turned out to be a stroke of luck for Esmellia. Not only is Fleance a rare bird who can actually keep up with Esmellia's wit, but his generally spooky appearance draws the eyes of anyone who would otherwise notice her striking appearance. Together they bummed around the hostels of [[Birdfoot]] for a while, pursued some of Fleance's leads together, got mixed up with the [[Berendsen Family]], and even briefly collaborated with [[Alberta de Genne]]. Eventually, fearful of her family's reach, Esmellia and her new lover [[Prim Vosdottir]] agreed to move to [[Milton]] in order to better hide Esmellia's identity, and to start a life together and founded the Startled Starling Inn.
