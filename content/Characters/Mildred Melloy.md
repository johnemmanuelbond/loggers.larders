###### Female, [[Andradorans]], 52
###### Location(s): [[Harport]], [[The Citadel]]
A diminutive and flabbily built human woman, Mildred is nonetheless a force within the [[Harport City Watch]]. Her case record is a (suspiciously) unblemished string of successes. She speaks with a thick southern andradoran accent and frequently gesticulates with her hands. Under her green cloak and leathers she wears an ill-fitting pair of trousers and a soiled white shirt.
