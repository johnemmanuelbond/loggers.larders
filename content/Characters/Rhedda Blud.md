###### *Female, [[Orcblood]], 36*
###### Location(s): [[Harport]], [[Milton]]
An orcblood from [[Drudwythe]], Rhedda has been working for [[Berendsen Family]] for many years.

A gruff, but jolly woman, Rhedda's demeanor isn't instantly foreboding and she is very professional about her work. She wears a very well-kept breastplate with floral embellishments under a leather overcoat. A battleaxe hangs at her hip and a wooden buckler over her shoulder. Her eyes are a dark black, her lower tusks are tipped with gold, and the left side of her head is shaven. The rest of her hair is braided on the back and is jet black with streaks of silver dye. Through her nose and ear are pierced long silver bolts that give a strange magical aura.

While she harbors no ill will against her marks, her calm demeanor masks a ferocity that comes out in battle.
