The commander of [[Fort Solton|Solton]] soldiers in [[Milton]], he has been instructed by [[Harald Brightbrow]] to follow [[June Sturgil]]'s instructions.

Silvoy takes his job very seriously and strives to embody the righteousness and justice that earns the grace of [[Pan|Amat]]. However, he has been cursed with a bit of a nasaly voice.

Silvoy is a large man with pale skin, light brown hair, round features and a nose that looks like it's been broken many times. He wears full-body chain mail under the grey and gold regalia of Fort Solton, and has a shortsword attached to his belt. He has a very loving relationship with his horse, Strongboy, who is a beautiful jet black stallion with warm brown eyes from the Solton stables.

Silvoy actually has a working relationship with [[Old Gran Mimsdottir|Old Gran]] on account of having to take care of his very pretty horse.
