###### *Female, [[Harport Colonials]], 42*
###### Location(s): [[Harport]], [[Andradora]], [[Guild Quarter|New Haven]]
Kaire has been [[Manfred Mackle|Manfred's]] first mate aboard the [[Skipping Seal]] for about 5 years now. Having made herself invaluable as a crew member she quickly rose up the ranks due to ambition and confidence. She has little respect for Manfred anymore as she perceives his flamboyance as wasteful friction. No better than Manfred when it comes to unsavory dealings, she has spotted an opportunity to discredit her captain and assume the role herself. Kaire is a tall dark-skinned and muscular human woman with silver hair and an eyepatch. She wears all an all brown set of leather trousers and a salt-stained linen undershirt. A long dagger is conspicuously sheathed on the small of her back, and an odd contraption hangs from her hip.
