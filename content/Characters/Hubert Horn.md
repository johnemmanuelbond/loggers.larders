###### Male,[[Andradorans]]/[[Gnomes]], 301
###### Location(s): [[Harport]]
Inventor and head operator of Syng's Barge in Harport.

A truly ancient looking gnome who lives in a squat and square stone tower on the north edge of the Birdfoot.

His living quarters is comically large for a gnome, but riddled with drawing papers and correspendance.
