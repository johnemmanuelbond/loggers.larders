###### *Female, [[Aire Nomads]], 39*
###### Location(s): [[Fowl Camp]]
Co-leader of the Aire in Fowl Camp, Vos is a seeress who has visions of the reborn sea spirit. Her views gained her followers among the Aire with sea-longing, but eventually got her expelled from Northern Aire society. She is currently in a rocky patch w.r.t her relationships with her daughter, [[Prim Vosdottir|Prim]], on account of the latter's romance with [[Esmellia Leek]].
