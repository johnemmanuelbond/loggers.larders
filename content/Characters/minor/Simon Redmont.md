###### *Male, Harport Colonial, 44*
###### Location(s): [[Harport]], [[Harport Racetrack]]
A well-to-do lesser noble in Harport, Simon has an air of self-importance and pretention.

Simon manages the staff and books at the [[Harport Racetrack]], ensuring the casino always turns a profit. Initially he and [[Frank Horibert]] were business partners, but when Frank inherited the track from his father Simon fell by the wayside.

Simon wears a well-tailored maroon waistcoat and black trousers. Pined to his lapel is a piece of silver jewelry depicting two dancing swans reaching for the sun, represented by an inlaid topaz.

If asked, Simon is all to happy to share that he's descended from the Barons of [[Brightbrook]] back in [[Andradora]], and is prepared to get into a whole spiel about the history of the region in the foothills south of [[Silverspire]], how the third son of the family, his father Phillip, emigrated to Harport out of a sense of adventure and to bring fame to the family.
