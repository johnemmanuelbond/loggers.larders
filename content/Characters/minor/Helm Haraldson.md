###### *Male, Aire, 41*
###### Location(s): [[Nordwythe]], [[The Aire Tundra]], [[The Floes]]

Helm Hardrasson has had a vision from the star maidens. It's time to reclaim the wood! Helm is a little bit of a spiritual zealot, big into traditional aire religion, distrustful of water, etc.

Goals:
1. Prove the taiga is survivable
2. Find evidence that the blight is lessening
3. Locate the Starsign Mirror, as seen in a vision
