Captain of the Sashelas' Fins, a fishing/trading boat out of [[Harport]]. They are going to Weir's point in order to get some open-sea tuna before the weather turns foul. Lethidious is the on of a famous [[Andradora|Andradoran]] adventurer and, while modestly competent, is a little bit of a nepo baby. His mother passed away last year and he's trying to live up to her example. While his sailing skills are well-honed, his shrewdness and business-canniness betray his lack of experience.

35 year old human with olive skin (flecked with silver) and short auburn hair He wears hoopy earings and a flamboyant purple vest  on Sashelas' Fins, a medium-sized fishing schooner. 

Knows: The skipping seal is the last big voyage back to andradora, its captain is [[Manfred Mackle]] (who promised Lethidious an interview upon their return). Lethidious is excited about it and will tell anyone that listens.
