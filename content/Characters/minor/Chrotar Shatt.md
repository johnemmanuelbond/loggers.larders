###### Male, [[Orcblood]], 67
###### Location(s): [[Harport]], [[Birdfoot]]
An aging bugbear from the gladiator pits in northern [[Drudwythe]].

Conspicuously missing an arm, his raspy voice comes unwanted out of a mottled brown cloak. Standing 6'2" while hunched over, Chrotar is a large and lanky bugbear, with furred flesh hanging limp over his emaciated form. In his heyday though, he was known as "Knave Cleaver" and occupied the champion's position during [[Petunia]]'s youth. Somehow, Chrotar recognizes Petunia after all these years, even though he was dethroned and cast aside before Petunia's own rise to prominence.

After his ousting, Chrotar traveled to [[Nordwythe]] via dwarven-drudwythan channels (similarly to Petunia) and entered the employ of the [[Berendsen Family]] as an enforcer. Even one-armed he was formidable until his age got the better of him and he was ruthlessly cast aside. After this slight he ended up joining the [[Spearfishers]] on a few raids in his twilight years, contributing useful insider knowledge. Now, well past his prime, he helps the organization as a subtle recruiter on the beggar's bridge.
