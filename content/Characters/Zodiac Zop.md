###### [[Sphinxes|Sphinx]], [[Orcblood]]/[[Andradorans]], very old
###### Location(s): [[Fish Market]], [[Harport Night Market]]
An ancient looking goblin, Zodiac Zop has saggy green arms that protrude out of a red and gold vestament. He runs a peculiar fortunetelling business out of a 4-post bed on wheels at the [[Harport Night Market]].

(DC 15 religion check, 12 for [[Andradorans]], to recognize the vestaments of [[Saints of Scaathiel]], a religion from [[Andradora]] currently dedicated to the worship of the [[Mage-King]])

Zodiac Zop's real name is Janus Pence

### Zop's Riddles
**Palm Reading**: (bs) 1sp, (zop's hands are extremely dry and yet but smell like bananas -- because he has been eating bananas)

**Fortunetelling**: (1st level) 1gp, Zop asks a bunch of BS questions and then casts a spell (like detect magic/identify) to glean information to make an educated guess on a fortune. He'll also identify stuff for 1gp if the customer is magic-savvy.  

**Minor Divination**: (2nd level) 10gp, Two PCs have to extend their palms and touch (one up, one down). Between one set of hands lies weal (white goat statue) and between another lies woe (black tiger statue). after some magic chanting and shit only one statue will remain. Alternatively, Zop can slightly modify locate object to get a compass direction for a magical essence.  

**Major divination**: (4+) 100+ gp  
Zop lays pulls out a literal crystal ball... only to open the lid and pull out a few items: A box full of gold dust, a box full of pitch, and a beautifully shining cyan, white, and amber statuette. The statuette has a feline figure with a human head and antlers. Laying out a piece of parchment, Zop uses magic to heat up the pitch, dips his finger into the hot pitch, then into the gold dust, and then he traces out an elegant magical rune (DC 20 arcana to tell that it's unlike anything they recognize from merfolk stuff) onto the parchment. Placing the statuette onto the parchment Zop directs the customer to look into the statue's eyes as he chants:  
"the door was made, the price we paid. and light burst through, with shadows too. It saw, it came, our gate it claimed. It sat, it reigns, till end ordained."

As Zop chants, the PCs start to feel dizzy and the room starts spinning as they can't take their eyes away from the statue. The gold dust heats up and eventually explodes outward, dazzling the occupants with light, filling the room with the scent of pine and metal. PCs make a DC 15 con save or get kinda high...

Zop's body banishes and the statue's eyes gleam gold. Wordlessly. As if booming through the room and yet piercing your mind's eye, the PCs hear: "Ask"

"Where is [[Esmellia Leek]]?"  
On hero's bank  
At thunder's yard  
In driver's den  
By flower's guard
