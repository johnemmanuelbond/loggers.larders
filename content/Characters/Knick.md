###### *Male, [[Harport Colonials]], Dead (21)*
###### Location(s): [[Milton]], [[Harport]], [[Birdfoot]], [[Harport Night Market]]
Born and bred in [[Harport]] Nigel Fleck worked his way up from street urchin to a respected member of the [[Berendsen Family]]. A rather nondescript man, Nigel, who goes by Knick in most circles, has ruddy red hair and pockmarked skin. His bright green eyes dart to and fro around any room he enters. He seldom so much as removes his hands from the grey woolen cloak surrounding his body, though a sly peek underneath will reveal leather armor and very many knives. Unlike his partner [[Rhedda Blud|Rhedda]], Knick is motivated by a strong drive to prove himself and acts quite ruthlessly to achieve his goals.
