###### Female, [[Andradorans]], 37
###### Location(s): [[Harport]]
The eldest daughter of the [[Robert Berendsen|first Berendsen]] to arrive in [[Harport]], Ophelia is a force. An extremely tall woman with cat-like features and long fingernails, Ophelia is usually dressed to the nines. Most often seen in a Yellow waistcoat over an extremely dark red dress, Ophelia travels around the city in a private carriage, flanked by guards, as she attends business.

#### Magical Abilities
Ophelia is a CR 9 (7th level spells) conjurer wizard (with a sick-ass summon).
