###### *Female, [[Andradorans]], 37*
###### Location(s): [[Harport]], [[Harport Racetrack]], [[Old City]]
The eldest daughter of the [[Robert Berendsen|first Berendsen]] to arrive in [[Harport]], Ophelia is a force. An extremely tall woman with predatory features and immaculately sharp pearlescent fingernails, Ophelia is usually dressed to the nines. Most often seen in an Ochre waistcoat over a royal blue dress, Ophelia travels around the city in a private carriage, flanked by guards on horseback, as she attends business.

![[Pasted image 20250521162324.png]]
#### Personal Goals
Beyond advancing the general power and prosperity of the [[Berendsen Family]] organization, Ophelia has an interest in continuing her appointment to the closet counsel of the [[Harport Diet]] so that she can divert [[Harport City Watch|Watch]] attention away from her laundering locations, as well as give legitimacy to clandestine meetings with [[Thomas Smythe]]. 

Ophelia is not particularly motivated by love and affection as much as control. While Thomas Smythe is an attractive partner at times, she keeps him at arms reach. Instead she has a certain [[Frank Horibert]], and his lucrative [[Harport Racetrack|Racetrack]], wrapped around her finger. A little marriage here or there never hindered a woman's goals...

Additionally, Ophelia also desires to build around herself a community of the city's best artists; and so she has an amicable personal contact with characters such as [[Lone Star State|Silvie Gram]] and [[The Caverns|Drumph, the Ogre Bard]].

#### Magical Abilities
Ophelia is a CR 10 (7th level spells) sorcerer (with a [sick-ass summon](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:summon-draconic-spirit)).
Her spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 17, +9 to hit with spell attacks). Ophelia can cast *Charm Person* and *Detect thoughts* at will and has the following sorcerer spells prepared:
- Cantrips (at will): _ray of frost, dancing lights, mage hand, prestidigitation, shocking grasp, shape water_
- 1st level (4 slots): _charm person, shield, ice knife_
- 2nd level (3 slots): _detect thoughts,  misty step_
- 3rd level (3 slots): _counterspell, slow_
- 4th level (3 slots): _greater invisibility, dimension door_
- 5th level (2 slots): _summon [[Cahuatelle]], dominate person_
- 6th level (1 slot): _Otiluke's freezing sphere_
- 7th level (1 slot): _etherealness_
