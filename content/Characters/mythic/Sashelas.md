###### *Male, [[Original Diaspora]], Dead*
###### Location(s): [[The Glinting Straits]]
Believed to be a demigod son of the sea ([[Pan|Morian]]), Sashelas was a sailor, and later sea-king, who sailed the glinting straits, the warm waters between [[Mardwythe]], [[Sundwythe]] and the island of [[Wydmont]]. His charge was maintaining the balance within the oceans, and between the various seafaring peoples of the straits. His reputation was more mixed amongst the inland civilized societies, as his influence curbed overfishing and limited expansionist states. This reputation changed when a renegade [[Sanguinistic Sects|Blood Mage]] unleashed a leviathan on the straits in a bid for political power. Sahselas rammed his boat into the leviathan, and slew it with holy magic. However he ended up swallowed and drowned as the leviathan sank beneath the waves. For this deed, his constellation "the whale and the ship" sits central in the southern sky.
