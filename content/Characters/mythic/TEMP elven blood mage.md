###### Male, [[Northern Elves]], very old
###### Location(s): [[Shimmering Grove]], [[Low Taiga]]

An ancient Elven blood mage, TEMP has bound his body and spirit to the tree at the [[Shimmering Grove]].

His body sits preserved in the cavern underneath the __ tree, in a state of perpetual decay as it is ever-so-slowly torn apart and sundered by the tree's roots.

The position of the __ tree between [[Eitheloth]] and [[The Floes]] allowed __ to exert his will on the inland waters of [[Nordwythe]]. Using this influence, __ proved to be a war-ending weapon for the Elves. By spreading a blood-plague in the river Wyfloe, __ cut off the [[Ancient Merlings|Merlings]]' ability to project power inland, and from then on the Elves were in a dominant position on Nordwythe.

While connected to the tree, TEMP's spirit is free to roam wherever the tree's roots touch, which is most of southwest Nordwythe. However, this shade spends most of his perusing/haunting the [[Ruined Tower]], remembering the days of his splendor and handling any intruders that would plunder his works.
