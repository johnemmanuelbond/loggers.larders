###### *The One True god*
The jealous and sadistic protector of the world. From its throne on the gas giant Panain it reigns as lord and holds closed the door to the shade beyond the shades. It is without fear, or shame, or remorse.
- Pan, Baphomet imagery for chaos, horns, hedonism,
- Baal, Satan,
- Janus imagery
- Watches the shaded doors
- Jupiter/God imagery
- tyrant
- source of power (lightning)
- hold the world captive (gravity)
- protect the world from meteors (beyondlings)
- Tidal friction gives energy

## Pan's Five Faces
### Melian
- Life, land, blood, chaos, renewal  
- Clerics: Life, Nature, Tempest
- Warlocks: Undying
- Warlocks: Fiend, Genie,
- Faces: sashelas, dragon of chaos,

### Morian
- Death, Waters, peace, travel
- Clerics: Death, Grave, Peace
- Warlocks: Undead, (Undying), Fathomless, Genie
- Faces: Charn, spirit of the sea

### Amat
- Day, authority, righteousness, war
- Clerics: Light, War, Order
- Warlocks: Fiend, Celestial,

### Scaathiel
- Night, patience, sloth, decay
- Clerics: Arcana, Knowledge,
- Warlocks: Hexblade, Archfey, Old One,
- Faces: shrub-hath

### Thuul
- Spirits, deception, ambition, fire
- Clerics: Twilight, Trickery, Forge
- Warlocks: Old One, Archfey, Fiend
- Faces: sky spirit

- The world of bodies - The world of spirits
- Melian - Morian
- Life - Death
- Earth - Sky
- Andradora - Tomme
- Balkai - Eitheloth
- Merchants - Mages
- Blood Magic - Spirit Magic
- Land - Sea
- Embodied - Beyondlings
