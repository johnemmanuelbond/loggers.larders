###### *Nonbinary, [[Aire Nomads]], 28*
###### Location(s): [[Milton]], [[Harport]]
Chef at the [[Startled Starling Inn]], Prim is the measured foil to [[Esmellia Leek|Esmellia's]] exuberance. A powerful-looking Aire nomad, she appeared with Esmellia about 15 years ago when they moved to town. In reality, she is the estranged daughter of [[Vos Flyggdottir]] who fled from her mother's religious movement after they were exiled from the Aire tundra. This makes her cousins with [[Kargoe Bepbiep Trukgohson]] Prim met Esmellia and [[Fleance Flycatcher|Fleance]] drinking at a tavern in [[Harport]], the three have been close friends ever since. Prim and Esmellia, in particular, struck up a romantic relationship before founding their Inn in sleepy Milton.

#### Local Politics
[[Wilson Fit]] is holding back pressure from the AEC to produce more lumber. He's been able to negotiate with the tempestuous [[Moser Wilderly]] and so has approached prim to help set up a trade deal with the southern Aire for extra logs\/guides. Maybe a new settlement is in the works.
