###### Male, [[Aire Nomads]]/[[Northern Dwarves]], very old
###### Location(s): location
An ancient Aire/Dwarven mixed blood. In days of yore, Mork made a pact with a vicious, but honorable, spirit of stone and rock. His ferocious patron acts as a foil to Mork's gentle nature. This combination turned him a folk hero in the minds of the ancestral Aire slaves under the [[Ancient Merlings|Ancient Merling]] empire. Representing a stoic connection to the land and an understanding that freedom is inevitable, Mork did many great deeds throughout a long (extended) life. Eventually his recklessness caught up with him and some spiteful Merling hired a powerful [[Old Hag|Hag]] to deal with him. Centuries later, this hag was defeated and destroyed by the combined efforts of [[Harken Keelmender]] and [[Shae]], and Mork's prison came into the ownership of a certain [[Grendelle the Grotesque]]

#### Appearance
Very short for an Aire, Mork only stands at sbout 5'2". He is, however, extremely broad and stocky, even for a dwarf. He, it (?), wears thick and bell-bottomed leather trousers, below which his feet seem to disappear into the ground. Above his waist his broad torso is only covered by a vest that looks like it was fashioned from layers and layers of deerskin. His exposed chest and arms are covered on a thick curls of brown and grey hair to the point where only some of his characteristically Aire stone-grey skin perks through. His bald head is covered in a series of geometric tattoos depicting age-old stories of the Aire great spirits defeating their mother the chaos dragon. In battle Mork often transformers into a more beastial form: His hairy chest gets even thicker to the point where it can resist some blades, his nails and teeth also extend significantly, and the debris and dirt start to swirl around his body.
