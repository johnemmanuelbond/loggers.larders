Sphinxes were created at the height of the [[Ancient Merlings]] civilization by a human mage who, cursed with the foreknowledge of [[Pan]]'s arrival, created a creature to collect and protect knowledge of the world. This mage created 31 such beings, and seeded them in various locations around the world.

Awakening during the blooming of peoples, the sphinxes have been guiding the reemergent humanity towards enlightenment over the last several centuries. Over time, many of the sphinxes met up and formed an [[The Knowing Shepherds|organization]] to embody their goals. While their organization persists to this day, the sphinxes themselves have been far less lucky. Upon the rise of the [[Andradora|Andradoran]] [[Mage-King]], the sphinxes represented a clear and present threat to his hegemony. 16 sphinxes were slain and consumed before the rest fled into hiding. Those that are left guide their organization from middle-management, protect what knowledge they've saved, and mourn their kin.

Sphinxes are a particularly noble form of chimera, with a human head adorned with majestic antlers and crowned in rainbows. Their feline body has cyan, white, and amber fur which glistens like gemstones.
