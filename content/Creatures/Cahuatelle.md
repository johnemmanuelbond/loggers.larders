[[Ophelia Berendsen|Ophelia]] dramatically pulls out the sapphire hairpin from behind her head and sings "come cahuatelle, show them the light". The wind in the area starts whipping as streaming out of the hairpin comes 5, 10, 20, feet of blue-scaled and yellow-feathered serpentine body. A brightly colored feathered mantle unfurls from the back of it's golden-crested head in shades of red and purple and gree. As it turns to face you with piercing white eyes lightning crackles from around its body as its beaky maw opens and lets forth a shrill cry, followed by a bassy gurgle, and the air begins to chill.
