"Come united light, shine on sanguine ripples, lay bare my enemies’ bones," reads a cursed verse stolen from the tomes of Elven blood mages. It is an apt description of Skinsplinter vines. Our enemies created these vicious constructs to rend the flesh from the armies of my people, and to “liberate” the villages of our slaves. In all my correspondence I have heard said that only the servile spiritualists possess the ability to gentle these verdant horrors. Though fire and blight have also proven effective in times of war. Mercifully, Skinsplinter seeds are magically inert unless watered with the blood of a [[Shay-Luuq]], a kind as rare as they are dangerous.
