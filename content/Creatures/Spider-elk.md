A mythical monstrosity with 8 spindly legs and huge antlers. An [[Aire Nomads|Aire]] hero was said to have slain one and used it's pelt to control the wind. In truth, the beast is still out there.

The 32nd sphinx, and the only seeded on [[Nordwythe]], the spiderelk was discovered by [[Ancient Merlings|Merling]] despots and transformed into a shay-luuq.
