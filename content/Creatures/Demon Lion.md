###### Parents: [[Shay-Luuq]],[[Snowcat]]
The demon lion has a deep purple coat, black mane, and green eyes. It's lips stay sealed until it bites with a grotesque maw of long spiney teeth. White boney spines emerge at points from its coat, but a collection of them encircle it's tail which ends in a skittering and whistling rattle of sorts. The rattle can be harvested and will function 3 times as on the statblock.
