#### Ophelia's Bodyguard
An intensely muscular 8-foot tall humanoid with pale pale skin speckled by green, like that artisanal pistachio ice cream. They wear a calf-length skirt, or tartan?, made of black and blue layered woolen cloth with red embroidery, above this garment they are shirtless with stone-like muscles. It wields a long, [[Vengeful Vigil|two handled sword]] with a wicked curve. About 2/3 up the broad blade there are three holes with silver rings dangling from them. However, the most striking part of it's appearance is it's face, or at least where there would be a face. Instead it seems to acknowledge you through a solid steel plate with an metallic shine of black and blue and red, like oil in a puddle. The designs swirl and convalesce over the faceplate into the vague implication of demonic face.

#### History
One of the premier exports of the [[Shadebinders of Tomme]], these beings are champion pit fighters who have been bound to a [[Beyondlings|spirit of voilance]] from planes unknown.
