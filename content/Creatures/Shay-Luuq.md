Via [[Ancient Merlings|ancient Merling]] shadebinding, a [[Beyondlings|beyondling]] spirit is taken and imprisoned within a physical form, usually an animal or plant. This inescusable affront to nature mutates both the host and the spirit, until both are misshaped beyond recognition.

Mutations usually take on an aquatic flavor, i.e. a lion with an angler-fish lure or a stinky stinky pelican.
