###### Parents: [[Shay-Luuq]]
A grotesque bird mutated by [[Grendelle the Grotesque]]'s magic. Mau be encountered in the [[Milton Forest]]. This pelican is huge, with a a 7ft wingspan. The pelican has the same signature purple-oceanic mutation as the moose and the lion. Big mucusy yellow eyes, white feathers with purple roots, a kinda oily secretion on its skin. The pelician beak is distended and exaggerated and sits on the end of a thick muscley neck with various globules hanging off it like a rooster's wattle.
