A grotesque beast created by a formidible hag, The Dretch is composed of three head-like blobs stuck together with slime, each with a set of stout webbed feet. Each "head" is jet black, but the slime is a sickly green. The top head has a gaping, gummy maw and smacking lips, a heavy brow, bright yellow eyes, and one sputtering nose-slit. Horrifyingly, slaying the Dretch only breaks it into three Dretchlings.
