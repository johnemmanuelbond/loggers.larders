## Overview

The Aire Nomads are a rugged tundra people with some isolated communities living in the southern forests and mountains. The Aire practice ancestor and spirit worship, have very limited contact with the Taiga elves, and have a deep but clouded cultural memory of domination under the [[Ancient Merlings]], causing them to fear the sea. The Aire distinguish between the light-wood and the dark-wood, and fear to venture where hags dwell.

The Aire primarily herd [[Aire Aurochs]] and [[Saber-Deer]] for milk and meat. They live in igloo villages in the summer but migrate to their coastal caves during the winter. In the coastal caves they grow brine mushrooms and tubers to supplement their diet.

## Central Myth

The Wind spirit and Water spirit, twin sons of the chaos dragon, turned on their mother and fought her so that order could rule the world. They succeeded and buried her on the other side of the world, where she still causes earthquakes and volcanoes.

However, the great spirit of the sea took a mortal wound, and where his blood spilled the Aire were born, and his body was destroyed. His will, however, endured, and it's by his power that the chaos-dragon is chained below ground. ## Demigods

Ansou: Spider-spirit who swindled fire from the very maw of the chaos-dragon to gift the Aire (among many other accomplishments)

Posi: A small fat Lemming-spirit who guides souls from the world of the living to the kingdom under the ground. He only comes out at night and is usually joking and jovial. Ansou regularly bamboozles him.

The star-maidens: 7 daughters of the sky spirit who hold the mirrors or truth so that their father never forgets what he must protect.

## Heroic Figure 
An Aire warrior-maiden once hunted the formidable silver spider-elk who slayed and dismembered the chief and his prized Aurochs. Upon finding the beast she slew it with an arrow tipped with a potent fungal toxin from a cave she found on the coast. Afterwards she skinned the silver coat from the [[spiderelk]] and could thereafter fly far across the tunder using it's wind-magic.

## History with Harken
After a few more journeys and the establishment of weir's point, [[Harken Keelmender]] formed a deep friendship with the southern Aire who he encountered, even marrying one. This friendship was formed off a promise that Harken would use his smithing skills and [[Andradora|Andradoran]] magic to help the southern Aire defend against a fearsome hag. Harken knew his strength couldn't last forever and so, before he was too old, he led a expedition to find the hag. This expedition was successful and the hag was slain, but Harken lost his life in the process. Unbeknownst to all, the hag's daughter escaped to cause trouble in the future.
