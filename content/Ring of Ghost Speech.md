*A silver ring with a hexagonally cut purple gem*

While wearing this ring, your skull is faintly visible though your face when in direct moonlight.

You may cast the *False Life* spell at will (at 1st level).

Additionally, once per week you may take 3d6 necrotic damage to cast *Speak with Dead* at 3rd level without expending a spell slot.

Finally, this ring may function as an arcane focus.

*Must be attuned by a creature who can cast 3rd level spells.*
