###### *Male, [[Aire Nomads]], 63*
###### Location(s): [[Harport]], [[Birdfoot]], [[The Pigeon Hole]]
The aging purveyor at the Pigeon Hole inn on the second level of the Birdfoot.

Rolf looks his age. While not decrept, the lines on his face and sag to his muscles are noticeable. He stands about 6'3" and is dressed in a faded blue tunic and brown trousers. His tattoos feature angular and geometric designs of a predatory big cat on one temple, a deer on the other, and the wind in between. 

Rolf has ran this little bar/hostel for about 20 years, beforehand he worked as a logget out in milton but doesn't have the strength of youth anymore.
