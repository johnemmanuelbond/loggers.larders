##### Explore
A strangely round and even meadow hidden within in the trees about 200 feet across. The ground undulates out in slow rolling grassy waves across the clearing. As you read in the letter, The scene is dominated by a large rocky ridge of gnarled granite and moss which reaches out from the treeline, slowly rising to about 30, 35 feet tall until it suddenly drops off a 35 foot cliff into the center of this meadow. Surrounding the ridge are a few tossed boulders jutting out of the otherwise peaceful grasses and slowly wilting dandelions, here in the dog days of summer. 

Alberta: *whispers* "On the other side of this ridge there's a crack leading inside, I don't know if the creature is still there, but stay on guard."
