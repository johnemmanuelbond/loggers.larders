### Aftermath

With [[Elga Elluin]] uninhabitable, the elves are forced to huddle in the caverns of [[Eitheloth]], dependent on the [[Northern Dwarves]] for trade and instruction on underground sustenance.

The [[Ancient Merlings]], on the other hand, suffered a blood plague so destructive they can no longer survive on land for more than a few hours at a time, forever breaking their hold on their human slave caste worldwide.
