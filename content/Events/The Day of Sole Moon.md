###### *August 30, 0223*
Each day a year the 4 moons line up in the western hemisphere (away from Pan's Disc). On this day, the light of the moons mix: Bright white (Amat), faint grey (Morian), pale blue (Scaathiel), and orange (Thuul). This mixed light makes colors appear more vivid and contrast appear more striking. Faint evanescent rainbows reflect off of exposed glass and metal. Additionally Pan's aurora is visible on the horizon, it looks like a gently undulating orange network of light-strands, shimmering and merging then separating and fading.

Cultures, like the [[Aire Nomads]], on [[Nordwythe]] and [[Drudwythe]] usually celebrate a harvest festival on this day, the autumnal equinox.

### Encounter
#### Prep:
During the banquet, Grendelle will sneak into town and into the belltower.

The (magically inert) skinsplinter seed is buried 5ft below the stage.

Judge Sturgil is hypnotized (DC 20 arcana check or DC 17 perception with detect magic) to throw the greatest festival ever. If broken, June will remember Grendelle, in disguise, carrying a full picnic basket, influencing her choice to send for extra AEC imports to "help improve Milton's visibility"  

Frieder is hypnotized to linger around the stage. If his hypnosis is broken, he will remember more details about Grendelle and the numerous hypnoses he's been under. He'll remember that Grendelle needs Milton on edge, though not why. He'll remember how she met a few times with a cloaked man in red gloves who delivered her a sealed missive. He'll remember the phrase skinsplinterer from their conversation, though not much else other than her cackle. He'll remember how he used to lure other lumberjacks off the trail and towards Grendelle. He'll remember how Grendelle would drown them in those seven pools and then trap their spirits within a magical barrier and merge them with something... else, something alien and deeply unnerving.

#### Combat 1:
At the end of the play and the height of the convergent moons Grendelle uses her mimicry to invisibly lets loose a high-pitched whistle. This will call the posessed pelican to Frieder's location. The overwhelming force of the town slays the possessed pelican (a shaay-luuq) its blood seeps into the ground where it is slain.

#### Combat 2:
The skinsplinter plant erupts from the ground (along with 5 tendrils) and it is on. Here's a tentative order of operations.  
- round 1: Jesper takes aggro while Silvoy, Gran, Esmellia, and Prim focus on evacuating children and civilians.  
- round 2: Jesper and Silvoy switch aggro, Gran and lumberjacks retreat, Esmellia and Prim find Fleance and make a plan. Rhedda (and knick) exits the tavern and appears to join the fight. From the belltower Grendelle uses the amped-up sea spray threespear (revealing herself).  
- round 3: Soldiers continue. Prim and Fleance charge Grendelle, Esmellia uses fire magic (if bidden). Rhedda approaches Esmellia.  
- round 4: Grendelle casts a spell, Soldiers continue, Rhedda and knick kidnap esmellia. Prim and fleance only see at the end of their turns.  

Should the skinsplinter plant successfully transform, Grendelle will attempt to shackle it to her will: 
- To start: "Lasto leithian o im rovail taur henion, gorthaur oer a dhaerlind hen o nîn-en-eithel."  *I speak the will of the Sleeper under Shimmer Grove, the fell Lord of Ashes and ancient Warden of the Floes.
- The skinsplinterer will not attempt to resist, and it is stunned during this. Grendelle will have to channel this for a whole round for it to succeed.

- Round 5: soldiers continue to attack skinsplinterer. Prim and fleance run after Rhedda and knick, they fight. Grendelle finishes her channel:

Upon gaining control, the skinsplinterer will scoop up Grendelle and trounce off, heading northwest.
