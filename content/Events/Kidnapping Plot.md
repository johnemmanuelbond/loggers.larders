###### *August 30 - September 8, 0223*
By order of the [[Berendsen Family]], [[Rhedda Blud]] and [[Knick]] have kidnapped [[Esmellia Leek]] in order to fulfill the bounty put out by [[Marion Vespera]].

### Setup
During [[The Day of Sole Moon]], [[Rhedda Blud|Rhedda]] and [[Knick]] used the chaos to knock out and carry away [[Esmellia Leek]] during the only moment when [[Prim Vosdottir]] was preoccupied. In the night they fled, by horse, to the [[Harport]] race track, (meeting at the ag outskirts through sending stone) where the [[Berendsen Family]] has a safe house.

The next ship bound for [[Andradora]] leaves in 10 days. In this time they need to secure covert passage on the ship and prevent Esmellia's escape attempts.

### Knick's Plan
Since the [[Berendsen Family]] are aware of the [[Spearfishers]]' smuggling, Knick hopes to use this as cover. Work with [[Kaire Knobloch]] to organize a bust on board the Skipping Seal a week before it sets out. After the watch is finished searching the ship and finding evidence of the [[Manfred Mackle|captain]]'s involvement with the (actually pretty benign) smugglers, Kaire will use the opportunity to seize the captaincy. Then, with the aid of a few corrupt watchpeople, himself and Rhedda will sneak Esmellia onto the boat when the authorities least suspect it. Knick doesn't particularly care that this will spark conflict between the Spearfishers and the Berendsens, and Rhedda is just in it for the excitement.

### Day 1 Night Market Encounter
The players spot Knick across the night market, Knick spots the players

Prep (for each 2 minutes of dawdling, knick gets more prep time)  
- 1: Knick runs inside Moser's tent to recruit help and watch from afar (Stealth check, Persuasion check)
- 2: Knick pays off two goons (5 Gp each) to go harass the players: make fun of mood's spoons, ask for a song (mockingly)
- 3: Knick pays off a third goon to influence kargoe to go buy a glass eye in Sasha's tent  
  
Player options (inexaustive)
- Bribe the goons with more than 5Gp to talk about Knick
- Attempt to ignore/get through the goons: DC 13 intimidation/persuasion/deception, contested strength (goons have help action)
- Attempt to question/observe the goons: DC 12 insight. DC 15 perception
- Start a fight.

If fight: (on initiative 15)
- Round 1: civilians scatter
- Round 2: street is deserted. If goons do well: Knick joins. If goons do poorly: knick flees
- Round 3: Guards arrive, Goons flee.  

After fight:
- Rivka enters tent and locks eyes with Moser Wormwool
- Rivka runs away in wolf form, chased by Berendsen goons
- Knick stalks the party back to caverns

- Round 1: 
- Round 2: duck around a corner, players 'behind' don't see where he goes and _must_ use perception/investigation
- Round 3: tries to make an obstacle, players _must_ use athletics/acrobatics
- Round 4: finds a friend, if players catch up there are now two Harport Goons
- Round 5: 

Chase ends with whoever gets 3 successes or 3 failures first. If party succeeds enter combat between the Harport Goon and the first player(s) to get three successes. Other players arive a round later dependning on how many failures they get.
