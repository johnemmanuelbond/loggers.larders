###### Parent: [[Guild Quarter]], [[Harport]]

Wedged between two warefouses is a very little storefront, only about 15 ft wide, with a wooden sign dangling by one nail between the door and a peaked roof. As it sways to and fro, you can make out written in an obnoxious purple calligraphy the single word *Esoterica*.

Entering the door you immediately clock up and to your left a misty blue orb flitting back and forth seemingly scanning the hallway which extends for about 20 or so ft. Along the sides of the hallway are all these little shelves with small toys and baubles and dolls proudly displayed, each with a tiny white slip of paper with a gold piece price on it, attached by a thin metal wire. Following the hallway leads you to a larger room similarly lit only by a single candle in the back left corner. From what you can make out of the shelves in the extremely dim light, the meticulously arranged and displayed items continue to surround this 20ft square room.

In the middle of the room you see a woman reading a book, which is surprising considering the darkness. From behind the book come two long and thin and yellowed drooping ears which sit beneath a tuft of curly white hair. These ears are attached to a woman who, putting down her book, excitedly looks up at you with eyes just completely bugging out of her head. Your recognize wide-rimmed glasses with an exceptionally thick lens give the effect that Ms. Magpies eyes take up most of her face. And what eyes indeed! they seem almost slitted like a cat's. She addresses you with a drawn out musical whisper of a voice: "ah wonderful, customers!".
