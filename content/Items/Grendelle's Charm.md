*A mummfied ravens foot sits tied to a necklace made of dried sinew*

While wearing this pendant, you can attempt to *hypnotize* (not a charm effect) a humanoid within 10 ft. The creature must make a DC 16 wisdom saving throw. On a failure it is incapacitated for a minute, until it takes damage, or another creature uses an action to wake it up. At the end of the minute the creature makes a DC 22 wisdom saving throw. On a failure the creature is *hypnotized* for a month. See GM for hypnosis details.

While hypnotized, the creature endeavors to fulfill a course of action specified during the hypnosis. The creature cannot be made to directly harm itself or allies. While hypnotized, the creature has no memory of being hyponotized, carries an almost otherworldly calm, has substantially reduced critical thinking, and has a shortened attention span for tasks that are not its specified course of action.

The hypnosis ends at the end of the month regardless of whether the course of action is complete or not. A successful *dispel magic* (DC 15),  or the destruction of this item can end the hypnosis early. Ending the hyponsis restores the creature's memory of the hypontist.

*SECRET* Each use turns the user's skin a little more green.
