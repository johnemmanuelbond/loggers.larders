*Tram, I wish I was writing with gladder tidings, though Vos' birds do bring some cheer to this dreary wharf. My son speaks the truth, in his own way. A **Granoch** preacher named [[Tievel Set]] has recently washed ashore in [[Weir's Point]] like a **laemon** of old, he is an evil omen for our people.*

*He claims to be from the faraway land and is here to bring his sun god to the us "savages" and clean our souls with holy fire. Most of the town ignores his ramblings, but he's garnered the protection of the local soldiery and I see more Granochs at his sermons every day. I write in hopes that you can rally our kin in the southern forests, and reestablish connections with the delta folk. I hope the Solton pass remains open, though I fear you may be monitored.*

*Your brother, Trukgoh*
