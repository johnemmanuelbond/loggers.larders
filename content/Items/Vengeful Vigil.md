*requires attunement*

+1 Greatsword

Blood for Blood: Once per turn, after you hit with a melee attack you may spend one hit die to deal 1d12 extra necrotic damage.

Retaliate: If you are hit with a melee attack within 5 ft., you can use your reaction to attempt to grapple the attacker *with the sword*. While the target is grappled, attacking any other creature ends the grapple on the target.

*A long, two handled dark steel sword with a wicked curve. About two thirds up the broad blade there are three holes with silver rings dangling from them.*
