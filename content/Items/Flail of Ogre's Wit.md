*requires attunement*

+1 flail. When you hit a creature, deal an extra 1d8 bludgeoning damage to each creature within a 5ft sphere. Alternatively, you can spend an attack on the ground to deal the same damage (no save).

While attuned, reduce your intelligence score by 1.
