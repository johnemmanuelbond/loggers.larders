### [[John Moore]]'s Vision

cosmic horror techniques
- Admission: State plainly that the thing is ineffable
- Circumscription: describe the effects rather than the thing itself
- Magnification: Compare the thing to something already extreme
- Alienation: Describe how it violates principles of reality
- Combination: use a combination of analogies to create a liminal effect
- Obfuscation: be vague and ambiguous
- Abstraction: use nonsensical descriptions
- Disorientation: reference alternative mind states
- Fictionalization: lie and exaggerate
- Omission: give no description
