*requires atunement*

While attuned, a creature  may take 2d8 necrotic damage to cast *Power word: pain* on one creature. The creature takes another 1d8 necrotic damage each round the creature is affected by the spell.

Additionally, Any creature attempting to attune to this spell also suffers *Power word: pain*.

*A ring of silver green mithril engraved with runes from the enchantment school of magic. Though plain looking, the edges of the band are decorated with an intricate design of miniaturized knotwork.*
