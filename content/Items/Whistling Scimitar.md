2/day. As a bonus action you acticate the scimitar. While active, the scimitar floats out of your hand and twirls through the air, responding to your thought. The scimitar gains a reach of 15 ft. and deals an extra 1d6 thunder damage on hit with a high-pitched whistle.

If you're not holding the scimitar, a creature a can use it's action to attempt a DC 20 dexterity check to grab the scimitar, which prevents you from attacking with it. If another creature grabs the scimitar, you can use your action to contest your intelligence against their strength to free the scimitar.

*A single bladed sword with a pronounced curve. While the blade is fine and sharp, the elaborate handle and guard contain numerous nooks and crannies for air to move. A blue sash hangs from the guard.*
