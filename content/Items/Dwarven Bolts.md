Cataclysm bolts

When fired from a crossbow, roll 1d6:
- 1-2: The bolt explodes, dealing 2d6 fire damage to creatures within 5 ft of the target (or half as much on a DC 13 dexterity save).
- 3-4: The bolt freezes, dealing 2d4 cold damage to creatures within 5 ft of the target (or half as much on a DC 13 dexterity save). Creatures which fail are also restrained.
- 5: The target takes 3d10 necrotic damage. If the creature is hit with a melee attack within the next round they must make a DC 13 constitution saving throw or be stunned
- 6: The bolt splits into 7 parts, unleasing a burst of shrapnel. Creatures within 15ft take 3d12 piercing damage (or half as much on a DC 13 dexterity save).
