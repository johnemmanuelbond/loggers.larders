*This small sphere of thick glass weighs 1 pound.*

If you are within 60 feet of it, you can speak its command word, *"Calya"*, and cause it to cast the *Light* or *Daylight* spell. Once used, the *Daylight* effect can't be used again until the next dawn.

As an action, you can speak another command word, *"Hilya"*, to cause the globe to float 5 feet off the ground until a creature grasps it. The globe follows you at a distance of 60ft if you move, taking the shortest route. If it cannot follow it deactivates.
