*A long silver trident with a barbed tip. the shaft is etched in the pattern of coiled eels.*

While attuned, this trident has the finesse property and a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls.

1/day: As an action, you strike the trident with another metal object. The trident booms with the crashing of waves and any creature within a 30ft cone must make a DC 14 constitution save or be deafened and charmed for a minute. While charmed, the creature is incapacitated and can't move. If the creature takes damage, or succeeds on a DC 12 wisdom save at the end of it's turn, the charm ends, though the deafen does not
