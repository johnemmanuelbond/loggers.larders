*requires attunement*

Versatile: Brass knuckles that deal 1d8 piercing or bludgeoning damage.

When you hit a creature, they must make a DC 12 constitution saving throw or be stunned for a minute. A creature can repeat the save at the end of each of it's turns. Once a creature saves, it is immune to the effect for 24 hours.

*A simple pair of brass knuckles with a ruby embedded in the palm grip of each hand.*
