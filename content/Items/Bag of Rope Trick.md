*While held in hand, this item looks like a simple red rucksack with a long rope tie like you'd hang from the end of a walking stick. When flapped out into the air, the bag flies up as high as it can, to a maximum of 45 feet up in the air, with the rope dangling below.*

*Climbing the rope allows entry into a 20ft cube. The space appears lined with creaky wooden planks and cobwebs. Four diagonal beams hold up the ceiling, and a faint whistling may be heard from cracks in the wood.*

_Rope Trick (modified):_
**Casting Time:** 1 action
**Range:** Touch
**Components:** V (Bagoa Teck), S (flap the item open), M (a magical rucksack)  
**Duration:** until dispelled or closed (Clossua Teck)

Using the item you utter the magic words, the bag then flies up in the air to a maximum of 45 feet and a rope dangles from the entrance per. At the upper end of the rope, an invisible entrance opens to an extradimensional space that lasts until the spell ends.

The extradimensional space can be reached by climbing to the top of the rope. The space can hold as many as eight Medium or smaller creatures. _At the DM's discretion, fewer creatures may fit due to crowding from items_ The rope can be pulled into the space, making the rope disappear from view outside the space.

Attacks and spells can’t cross through the entrance into or out of the extradimensional space, but those inside can see out of it as if through a 3-foot-by-5-foot window centered on the rope.

Anything inside the extradimensional space stays within when closed. However, it only contains enough air for 8 creatures for 1 minute (divvied up between as many creatures are present).
