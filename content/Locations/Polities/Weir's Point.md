A fishing town on the northeastern peninsula jutting into the frigid norther sea, Weir's point is several decades older than [[Harport]], even though it is eclipsed in significance. The harbor at weir's point is not deep enough for large intercontinental galleys.

#### Ruined Temples
Weir's point is the location of several buried [[Ancient Merlings|ancient]] temples to odd and frightful gods.
