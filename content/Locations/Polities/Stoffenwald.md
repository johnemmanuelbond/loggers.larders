###### Parent: [[Nordwythe]],[[Shimmer Grove]]
![[stoffenwald.png]]


A small village with just under one hundred inhabitants, Stoffenwald subsists via herding, foraging, and trade with occasional [[Northern Dwarves|Dwarven]] Caravans moving between [[Balkai]], [[Harport]], and [[Karrid]].

The village consists of three circles of large hide tents. The hides are supported by enormous poles of curved wood, carved into shape and embellished with naturalistic designs. You notice two varieties, the smaller tent is 30ft by 30ft, with four poles lashed together at the top. Clearly these tents are not merely for camping and seem to invite more permanent, multi-seasonal dwelling. The larger tent is 30 by 60 feet the largest tent by far, located just off-center to the village, is a massive octagonal, multi-panel construction that is 30 feet on each side and rises nearly 50 feet in the air. Each of the 8 side panels is an open entryway with only the massive wooden supports to either side. A steady plume of smoke billows from an opening in the top. Inside you can glimpse several tables and other pieces of rudimentary, functional furniture assembled around a central smoking hearth.

The village is currently absent any traders, but a hunting expedition has returned carrying a giant Roc egg. Torstan Halvardson has come of age and successfully braved the mountain home of the mighty Roc!

### Locations

#### Party Tent

Within the main tent you see a slew of Aire seemingly preparing a meal! There are several stations with village elders chopping foraged roots and mushrooms, a few fit-looking hunters carrying a tied up deer out one of the 8 doors, and there's even a gaggle of gossiping teens struggling to wash off various pieces of mismatched cookware.

Coordinating this whole thing is an older Aire man, (Halgrim Thrice-Woven). He must have been extremely formidable in his youth because even in his old age he stands at a respectable 6 feet tall and his tattoed head sits atop broad shoulders. In a grinding voice he gestures for those hunters with a three fingered hand and goes to inspect their kill.

#### Kiln

Outside, near the party tent, there is a patch of heavily, intentionally, trodden grassless ground whereupon two structures stand. Closer to the tent is the aforementioned rotisserie where a figure entirely cloaked in an enormous bearskin (Signe Bearskin) sits perched at the crank, slowly turning a skewered boar of some variety as it roasts over an open flame. Also in this patch sits a large, domed, clay structure with an opening near the bottom glowing with heat and a steady plume of black smoke rising from the  top.


#### Zodiac Zop

Mysteriously, [[Zodiac Zop]]'s cart (a four-poster bed on wheels) has posted up in the middle of Stoffenwald!

Succeeding on a DC 12 check (insight/perusasion) on the villagers reveals that Zop has only been in town for about two days, coming from the direction of the monastery. Zop was sent to Stoffenwald on request [[Ambrose Pence]] (DC 20 insight to at least get a clue).
