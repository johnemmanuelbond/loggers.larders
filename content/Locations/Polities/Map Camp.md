After about an hour of padding past floating rafts of muddy grasses and vegetable matter, you begin to see a trail of smoke rise out of the tall reeds from a point a little ways upriver. As you approach from the east around a bend in the river, you see the smoke is rising from behind a wooden palisade sitting atop a pad of packed mud and straw. You see about four upturned canoes in front of an opening in the palisade, behind which you see a little movement as a lithe dangerous-looking redheaded human and a striking 9 foot tall silver-furred minotaur trail behind a jovial halfling woman with a cute little canvas hat who is skipping through the gate straight at you.
