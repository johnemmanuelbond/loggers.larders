###### Parent: [[Nordwythe]], [[Lake Balkai]]

A ramshackle fishing and trading post on the north shore of the endlessly deep [[Lake Balkai]], Karrid really only has about three streets to fit a couple hundred wood-plank houses. That's a few hundred *intact* houses to be clear. For each functioning abode there are two burned husks, or one abandoned hovel replete with boarded-up cobwebbed windows. You see, most of the town's skilled population leaves Karrid behind to practice craft across the lake in the mountain city of [[Balkai]] under the auspices of the [[Flaming Conclave]] and the mass of resources at their disposal. Karrid is peripheral, forgotten, left to those without the skill or patronage to earn the rings in their beard. The town's denizens represent the lowest rungs in dwarven society, a town of grumbling fisherman wasting their talents fashioning crude nets to pull in the admittedly vast bounty of fishes living in the volcanically heated lake. A town of venturers and traders seeking to reestablish lines of communication with an ally not heard from in centuries. A town of penniless and hostile outcasts from the golden city just across the waters. Every month they gather at the local abbey to raise gripes with a governor who isn't even there most of the time. Every year they break the spirit of a batch of new clergy hoping to lift this place out of squalor. Some people find solidarity in this shared existence, but most don't.
