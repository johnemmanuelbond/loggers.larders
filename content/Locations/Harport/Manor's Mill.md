###### Parent: [[Harport]]
A tavern on the outskirts of the fisherfolk's residences and close to the [[Berendsen Family|Berendsen]] Manor, the manor's mill is the drinking hole for their security staff. Most of the rooms are filled by both guards their guests, but a room can open up for sufficient coin (2gp).

#### Staff

#### Patrons
Anor: Female Human Professional. Anor is exceptionally beautiful, with thin copper hair and amber eyes. She wears fine clothing and several pouches hang from her belt. Anor is easily distracted by small animals.

Lindie: Female Human Artist. Lindie has a narrow face, with straight silver hair and soft blue eyes. She wears tailored clothing and a silver holy symbol (Melian). Line is haunted by the memory of someone she saw murdered in the Birdfoot alleys.

Umun: Female Dwarf Soldier. Umun is short and thin, with copper hair and large green eyes. She wears scale mail and wields a spear and light crossbow. Umun holds a grudge against Orcs because she keeps getting beaten in the fighting pits under the Stroke of Luck in the redlight. She will actually recognize the trout pin and (maybe) the drawing of [[Rhedda Blud|Rhedda]]. Currently she works for the [[Spearfishers]] as eyes and ears at the Manor's Mill.

Athyew: Male Halfling Fisherman. Athyew has a square face, with brown hair and bright brown eyes. He wears tailored clothing and silk gloves. Athyew seeks a company of adventurers to clear his name against charges of stealing a pair of magic silver bolts?? from [[Sigurd Steamsinger]].
