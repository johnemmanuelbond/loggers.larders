###### Parent: [[Harport]]
Located on a small island between [[Harport]] proper and the [[Birdfoot]], the citadel manages most of the traffic between the two parts of the city. While the Citadel is home to the [[Harport City Watch|City Watch]] barracks and the Harport Prison, the island is still open to public life. In this quarter watchmen are busy moving supplies back and forth, going about their day-to-day. While the barracks complex is huge, the citadel itself retains a rather wide-open feel. Astride the Axel sits the Counsel's Campus, a wide field used both for City Watch drilling and\u00a0the location of annual Diet selections. The citadel itself has a few docks opposite from the [[Old City]]'s Old Haven, mostly reserved for government business. 
- POI: Barracks, Prison, Counsel's Campus.
