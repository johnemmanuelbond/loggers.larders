###### Parent: [[Harport]]
Mostly built up quickly after the big fire of 0125, the resulting neighborhood has basically no internal structure. Here we see a combination of two-story tenements, poorly demarcated hostels, family homes, [[Aire Nomads|Aire]] lean-tos, thatch huts, and a series of winding paths and lanes which connect the [[Guild Quarter]] to [[The Citadel]]. At the south end of Birdfoot, at the border of the [[Harport|Ag Sector]], looms the enormous racetrack, which doubles as a gambling den, and the best spot in town to broker deals. Next to the racetrack is Harport's red light district, complete with a series of bars and clubs right across the river from the Citadel. In fact [[Harport City Watch|watchpersons]] are this district's  most consistent clientele. While it's not obvious from the surface, the underlying limestone on the Birdfoot is ideal for delving secure underground shelters, and so the population here is even larger than expected.
