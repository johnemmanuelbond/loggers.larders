###### Parent: [[Harport]]
Confined within a curtain wall and connected to the citadel, the old city does not appear old at all. While this was indeed the site of Harken's original settlement, a fire in the year 0125 burned down most of the original structures. By then, Harport was rich enough to import both Dwarven and Andradoran architects. The resulting stonework is equal parts grand and civic. The buildings are very much intended to give off airs of community and governance, despite the rather ironic retaining wall which cuts the island in two. Regardless, here the streets are wide and lined with transplanted and manicured Egarthopian trees which shoot up periodically from the sidewalks. Stern-looking [[Harport City Watch|city watchpersons]] stand tall at each corner, so no active patrols are necessary. Finally, the Old City contains a relatively small dockyard called the Old Haven, reserved mostly for affluent passengers and clergymen travelling to treat with the [[Harport Diet]].
