###### Parent: [[Harport]], [[Birdfoot]]
Occupying the eastern edge of the Birdfoot, The Caverns Inn is a deceptively small building for its reputation. A one-story wooden building painted red and brown opens up to one large room with seating and a public bar. But the kitchen, and all the rooms, are downstairs. In fact, the Caverns is by far the city's largest hostel. Where most inns and hostels around Harport have at most 10 rooms, the caverns boasts just under 30 chambers of various sizes (and prices) delved into the seaside limestone. At full capacity, the inn can feed and sleep about 100 people. Descending down a long set of carved stairs from the reception area brings you to the main hall. This room has dozens of wooden tables, a drink bar and a buffet bar, and a stage. On the other end of the stage the room opens up into a huge window looking out over the mouth of Shae's Stream to Harport proper. Doors to the left and right of the stage exit to hallways and stairwells leading to the bedchambers, and doors to the left and right of the big window open to several smaller quiet sitting rooms with smaller windows which open to the bay. A cleverly carved series of wind tunnels utilizes the sea breeze to cool the rooms in the summer and heating them, via hearth, in the winter. In fact, the Stonehand family, who constructed The Caverns, are responsible for teaching the Footers the dwarven arts of delving. These underground dwellings were crucial for the population's survival of the 0125 fire, and for housing over half of the Birdfoot's current population. The service the Stonehands have provided the Birdfoot has made them virtually untouchable and revered throughout the community.

#### Menu
The Caverns puts on a buffet. It's awesome. there's basically every [[Harport]] staple. Baked fishes, custard pies, mushroom samplers, sunsquash soup, anything you can think of. It's 1 sp for all you can eat but 2 cp for a plate.

A mead or ale is 1 cp.

There's also genuine dwarven Firewine on request for 2sp/bottle. (Gives immunity to fear for an hour, but it's also a DC 12 Con save or be poisoned for an hour)


#### Staff
The Caverns' purveyor, a ancient dwarf named [[Ashan Stonehand]] was the first dwarf to take up permanent residence in Harport and even befriended [[Shae]] herself during the latter's twilight years. Between community organization and the everyday stresses of running Harport's biggest inn, Ashan is spread thin and getting older. Increasingly, she relies on her son [[Nerva Stonehand]] to run the Inn's day-to-day.

Ashan runs a tight ship, which requires a relatively large staff:

#### Soup Kitchen:
On Sundays The Caverns buffet transforms into a free food pantry for the [[Birdfoot]]'s needy. It is recommended that guests exit the premises for the morning hours as the doors to the rooms are all locked (for safety).
