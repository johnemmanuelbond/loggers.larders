###### Parent: [[Harport]]
Located on the tip of the [[Birdfoot]], the Guild Quarter houses the city's main industrial and commercial center. While the expertly masoned guildhalls that line the district's main avenues (1st, 2nd, 3rd, and Cross St.) are certainly the main draw of this district, it also houses the New Haven which is the city's biggest port. Nearly every building the in the Guild Quarter is a business of some kind, town criers walk the streets, advertising whichever businesses most recently bought their services. Since the security of this district is paramount to Harport's success, it is regularly patrolled by [[Harport City Watch|City WAtch]], [[Knights of Termat|Solton's Knights]], AEC security, and hired [[Morning Stars]], who (due to their common goal) coordinate intel at the Watchhouse.
