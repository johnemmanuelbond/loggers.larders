###### Parent: [[Birdfoot]], [[Harport]]
A dingy dive bar within the Birdfoot Tunnels, the pigeon hole has naught but a few regular patrons, most of whom are just personal friends with its owner, a crusty Aire named [[Rolf Ingridson]].

The cavern is dimly lit by a few braziers placed along at intervals along the walls and lanterns hanging from a network of suspended netting on the ceiling. A long L-shaped bar occupies most of the chamber, but there are a few tables towards the back, one longer banquet tables and two high-tops. ### Encounter

[[Rhedda Blud]] and [[Knick]] are waiting here to ambush the [[Player Party]] and try to get them to stand down. Rhedda will be nonchalantly sitting at the bar while Knick is hiding in the kitchens.

Rhedda is impressed with the party's capability, and thinks it would be a shame to waste them on an ignoble death. Any number of ventures in Harport could be wanting of their developing talents. Unfortunately they have no idea how to keep a low profile. She's here with an offer and a warning.
1. You can pay [[Esmellia Leek]]'s bounty and I'll let her go. Supply us 10k gold by Friday and she'll personally help bury any documentation that Ms. Landros ever came to Nordwythe. If you can't afford that. Otherwise consider if she's worth my axe to you. And don't bother trying to involve the watch, you couldn't possibly know how many of them are in our pocket, and one wrong move will cost you precious, precious time.
2. And you, [[Rivka|Wolf-Girl]], best you stay away from the track. It took us less than a day to track you down. I don't know if you're the same shapeshifter that... liquidated so much profit a few years back. But the owner is still looking for you. I'd turn you in myself, but you know... bigger fish.

If/when the party fights.
- Round 1:
	- Knick uses legendary action to restrain a bunch of party members with a crossbow bolt to the netting on the ceiling.
	- Rhedda downs a potion of strength (for athletics checks) and heaves the granite table ahead of the wall, blocking an easy escape, perhaps splitting the party.
	- On Knick's turn he either attacks or readies an attack against whoever Rhedda engages.
- Subsequent rounds:
	- Rhedda activates her echos and wreaks havoc.
	- Knick uses sneak attack on his turns,
	- Villain actions: flank to help maneuver Rhedda. Smokescreen if fight goes south.
