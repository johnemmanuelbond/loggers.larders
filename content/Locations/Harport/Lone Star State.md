###### Parent: [[Harport]], [[Birdfoot]]
Located at the border of the [[Birdfoot]] and the [[Guild Quarter]], the lone star state is known for it's dubiously successful concert schedule. Solo acts only.

The inn is a simple wooden shack, with unusually low ceilings. Accommodations consist of several wooden cots in the cellar and several hammocks in the common room.

#### Staff
Viggo (Owner): a nervous looking middle-aged halfling in a black waistcoat and trousers.

Silvie (Event Coordinator): an energetic and pretentious catfolk woman with an air for the dramatic. She has beautiful white fur underneath a slim green gown. She has taken kindly to [[Bartholomood]] despite his... quirks.

Ted (Cook): a gruff human with a thick black beard and hairy arms. Hates Bartholomood's guts after his (ex) girlfriend cheated on him with Bart (who didn't know).

Mary Akkery (Singer): The house act, and Bartholomood's rival, she is curvy fair-skinned human who wears tight black leather pants and a fluffy pink boa over a black blouse.

#### Patrons
Drarder Keson: Male Halfling Artist. Drarder has red hair and amber eyes. He wears well-made clothing and a silver holy symbol. Drarder quietly seeks to become a necromancer after escaping a cultish ceremony of the [[Grim Sages of Shub'hath]].

Bali: Female Dwarf Professional. Bali has tangled brown hair and bright green eyes, and a malevolent smile of sharpened teeth. She wears well-made clothing and a silver amulet. Bali is an agent of [[Spearfishers]] and would recognize [[Chrotar Shatt|Chrotar's]]

Sigo Roper: Male Human Soldier. Sigo has silver hair and sharp green eyes, and a pattern of unusual marks on his leg. He wears leather armor and wields a long sword and dagger. Sigo comes for every one of Mary's performances.

Becha: Female human Merchant. Becha has a round face, with short white hair and sharp amber eyes. She wears modest garments but carries a long knife. Becha dislikes anyone bigger than her.
