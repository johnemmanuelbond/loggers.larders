The swampy coastal forest surrounding the town of [location:4814537]\u00a0and encircling [[Milton]]. The forest has many denizens, not all of which are friendly.

#### Moose Corpse
The Corpse of a moose which, at a point, was [[Shay-Luuq|posessed by a fell spirit]]. The flesh around it's burst ribcage is purple and sloughing, though there is no flesh around it's mouth, which has morphed into the spiney maw of an angler fish. Dangling from it's chin is a now-dark lure-looking thing. It has two sets of antlers, one seems normal but the other is growing out of where it's eyes normally are, to compensate another pair of eyes has grown down the snout. It's hooves are split and bloody. On a good enough check they can tell the moose died spontaneously after rampaging through the forest. Crows feast on the decaying flesh, succeeding at a check reveals they know the location of the nearby Aire Camp. A very good check reveals that they also speak to the green woman on the other side of the mountain, she is good to them and they tell her much about the goings on in chopper's nest.

#### Saber-Deer
A trio of [[Saber-Deer]] (three sisters) makes it's way through the forest (think siberian musk deer, but with razer-sharp horns too). They'd be fun to talk to, but they don't really know a lot. The river has tasted weird recently. And one of their number was eaten by a very scary mountain lion.

#### Bears
A mama grizzly and two cubs stalk through the forest, the mama would defend her cubs but most likely run from a larger group of party members. If the party can talk with them, the mama knows to avoid the nearby Aire camp as well as the woods woman on the other side of the mountain.

#### Demon Lion
The party is stalked and attacked by a possessed mountain-lion. The lion will attempt to stalk the party, though the whistling of it's tail will be noticeable.

#### Cave
The party encounters a cave entrance, if they go in they find a slimy floor and are attacked by glowworms. One of the glowworms has a coin shining through it's belly. Flip the coin to get advice on a decision. player and dm roll, if the sum of rolls mod 3 equals zero the answer is right. Otherwise the answer is based on the dm's roll odd=tails, even=heads.

![[Lurid Worms]]
