The decrepit remains of [[Gilothy Silarin]]'s autonomon lay dormant in [[Ruined Tower#The Hold|The Hold]]:

The party must complete a skill challenge to reanimate it. The damaged encasement must be reforged, the scratched rune must be reengraved, and the [[animation-gem.png|animation gem]] must be incorporated into its latticework.

Appropriate skill sinclude sleight of hand to help repair runes, perception to identify weak spots, athletics to lever parts into place, obviously arcana is important. You're looking for 4 successes.

DCs: 14 (mundane), 19 (outlandish), +2 for each repetition

Each failure: Val's form takes 1d8 damage (for once it's reanimated)
3 Failures: Val is flickering to life but cannot move itself. Will require future work.
0-1 Failures: Val is reanimated with bonus abilities from the animation gem
- memory of the astrolabe
- animate objects as a spell
A nat 20 on any check can get one of these.

*Skill Challenge:*
1. A player may attempt a skill check.
2. 3 failed skill checks equals a failed skill challenge. This means consequences.
3. A player can only use skills they are proficient in.
4. Once a player attempts a skill they may not attempt that skill again.
5. Once a player attempts a skill, the DC goes up for other players.
6. The "help" action is allowed but you need to pass the current DC to actually invoke the benefit. All the prior rules apply, but the DC increase is postponed for the recipient.
7. Helping counts as your attempt at a skill.
8. Outside the box thinking (spells, benefits, etc) is highly encouraged.
