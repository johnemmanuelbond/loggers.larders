
To Lord Garthanel of Silarin

It appears I owe you a professional apology.

I have attempted, at some length, to reproduce the brass from our previous transaction. The results have been as satisfactory as they have been elucidating, which is to not at all. I must say I am impressed that any furnace in Eitheloth is capable of such subtlety.

So, as token of my sincere intent, I report to you that your alloy is of *exceptional* value to magicians, and there are those in royal circles who will see that value. This is all to say do not be afraid to ask a steep price.

In that vein, 

%%
In that vein, I am prepared to supply such a steep price for a limited quantity of the same material, produced and supplied with the same attention to discretion as our first correspondence. The use will be familiar in strain, if not in purpose. Compensation will be precise, and prompt.

I will send a courier with further specifications.
