**_You have made a pact with an unknowable and malevolent god who is, for whatever reason, interested in your mundanity. Such an entity offers phenomenal power over the world's base forces--light, mass, and the mortal heart--all in return for service in furthering its own inscrutable goals._**

### Apostate (special)
Starting at 1st level, you can use your wisdom, instead of your charisma, as your spellcasting modifier.

### Bonus Cantrips
At 1st level, you learn the [Thaumaturgy](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:thaumaturgy) and [Guidance](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:) cantrips. They count as warlock cantrips for you, but they don’t count against your number of cantrips known.

### Bolster Pawn
Starting at 1st level you can use a bonus action to target one creature you can see within 30 ft. That creature gains 1d8 + your spellcasting modifier temporary hit points which last for ten minutes or until you use this feature again. When you reach 10th level in this class the temporary hit points increase to 2d8 + your spellcasting modifier.

You can use this feature a number of times equal to your proficiency bonus, and you regain all expended uses when you finish a long rest.

### Revel in Domination
Your patron is eager to see you deprive agency from the useless rabble. Starting at 6th level, whenever a creature fails a saving throw against one of your *enchantment* spells, you have advantage on your next attack roll.
